Domestic Discovery

Welcome to Domestic Discovery!
Embark on a scientific journey at the home exhibit during the festival.
How to Play:
1. Decipher Clues: Decode hints leading to fascinating household items.
2. Guess the Marvel: Use clues to guess the object.
3. Unveil with Your Camera: Point your camera at the image trigger next to the object to reveal its cool science secrets.
4. Immerse in Discovery: Learn about the object's hidden scientific significance.
5. Hints & Rewards: Use up to 4 hints per level if needed, but each hint costs points. Earn mystery rewards and a chance to be featured on the Wall of Fame.
Ready to Unravel Science?
Remember, each level challenges you with science clues about an object. Guess right, use hints wisely, and point your camera to explore the cool science behind it.
